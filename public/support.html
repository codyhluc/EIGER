Eiger Support

Need help with Eiger? Email us at business@eiger014.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Include your account email and, if it's a bug, what you were doing when it happened.

Common questions

How do I manage or cancel my Eiger Pro subscription?

Open the app → Profile → Manage Subscription, or use your device's app store subscription settings (App Store: Settings → your name → Subscriptions; Google Play: Play Store → Payments & subscriptions).

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Profile → account settings → Delete Account. This removes your profile and associated data. You can also email us to request deletion.

Why does my readiness score look wrong?

Scores depend on the gear details you've entered (insulation, membranes, boot ratings). Check your gear specs in My Gear — and remember scores are informational only, not a safety guarantee.

How do I report a user or comment?

Every comment and profile has a report option; you can also block users from their profile.
