DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

Last updated July 19, 2026

You can permanently delete your Eiger account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how, what gets deleted, and what you should know before you do.

Delete in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the Eiger app:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

This in-app deletion flow is being finalized and ships in the next release. Once available, it is the recommended way to delete your account — it removes your data immediately and requires no wait.

Delete without the app

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by email:

  • Send an email to business@eiger014.com from the email address associated with your account.
  • Use the subject line “Account deletion request”.

We will complete the deletion within 30 days of receiving your request.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your account and all data associated with it, including:

  • Your profile
  • Saved mountains
  • Gear lists
  • Trips
  • Comments
  • Follows

What is retained

Nothing tied to your identity is retained after deletion. We may retain anonymized, aggregate data that can no longer be linked back to you, where permitted under our Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Eiger account does not cancel an active subscription purchased through the App Store or Google Play. If you have a paid subscription, cancel it separately in your App Store or Google Play account settings to avoid future charges.
