Privacy Policy

Eiger — operated by Eiger014 LLC · Effective July 15, 2026

Eiger is a mountaineering readiness app. This policy describes what information the Eiger mobile app collects, why, and the choices you have. The short version: we collect what the app needs to work, we don't run ads, and we don't sell your data.

Information you give us

  • Account details — your email address and name, used to create and secure your account (sign-in is by emailed one-time code).
  • Profile information you choose to add — such as experience level, body weight (used for gear guidance), bio, and home region. All optional.
  • Your content — gear lists, trip plans, climb logs, route condition updates, and comments. Your gear and plans are private to your account; profiles, climb logs you share, and route updates are visible to other users as the app indicates.

Location

If you grant location permission, the app uses your device's location while the app is open to suggest nearby mountains. Location is used on your device for that feature and is not stored on our servers or shared with other users. The app works without it.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no advertising trackers.
  • No sale of personal information — to anyone, ever.
  • No analytics profiling: the app contains no third-party analytics SDK.

Service providers

Like most apps, Eiger runs on infrastructure operated by service providers, who process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and storage for your account and content.
  • Mapbox — map imagery (your device requests map tiles, which involves your IP address, as with any map service).
  • Expo (EAS) — delivery of app updates.

If in-app purchases become available, payments will be handled by your app store (Google Play or Apple) and a subscription-management provider; we never see your card details. We may disclose information if required by law.

Security & retention

Data is encrypted in transit, and access to your content is enforced by row-level security on our database — other users can only ever read what the app deliberately makes visible. We keep your data while your account exists.

Deleting your data

Email business@eiger014.com from your account address and we will delete your account and associated personal data. An in-app deletion option is planned before public launch.

Children

Eiger is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Material changes will be announced in the app.
