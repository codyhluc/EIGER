Eiger Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Eiger ("the app") is operated by Eiger014 LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use the Eiger mobile app or eiger014.com, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

  • Account information — email address, name, and sign-in identifiers when you create an account (including via Google Sign-In).
  • Profile information you choose to provide — experience level, body weight (used for gear-fit calculations), avatar, bio, home region, and profile settings.
  • Your gear and activity — gear items you add, mountain readiness results, expedition logs, bookmarks, likes, comments, follows, and group-trip participation.
  • Location — only while you use the app ("when in use"), to show maps, nearby trails, and your position during navigation. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Purchase status — whether you have an active subscription. Payment details are processed by Apple, Google, or Stripe; we never see your card number.
  • Device signals — push-notification tokens (if you enable notifications) and crash/diagnostic data used to keep the app working.
  • Waitlist email — if you join the waitlist on our website.

How we use it

To provide the product: computing gear readiness for mountains, showing recommendations, syncing your data across devices, enabling social features you opt into, processing subscriptions, sending notifications you request, and fixing crashes. We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers

We rely on a small set of processors to run Eiger:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting.
  • Apple / Google — app distribution and in-app purchases.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status management.
  • Stripe — web payments (when purchased on our website).
  • Mapbox — map rendering (map requests include your device's IP address).
  • Sentry — crash reporting.

Affiliate links

Some product links in Eiger are affiliate links (via networks such as AvantLink). If you buy through them, the retailer may pay us a commission at no extra cost to you. Retailers may use cookies to attribute the purchase.

Sharing between users

Content you post (comments, expedition logs, public profile fields) is visible to other users per your privacy settings. Private profiles limit what others can see. You can block or report other users in the app.

Your choices and rights

  • You can edit or delete your profile information in the app.
  • You can delete your account (and its associated data) from within the app.
  • You can disable location access or notifications at any time in system settings.
  • For access, correction, or deletion requests, contact us at business@eiger014.com.

Data retention and security

We keep your data while your account is active and delete it when you delete your account, except where retention is required by law. Data is stored with access controls (row-level security) so users can only read what they are entitled to.

Children

Eiger is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We will update this page when the policy changes and revise the date above.
